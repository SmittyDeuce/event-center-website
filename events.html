Events

3 on 3 tournament(starts 2nd week of June)

Monthly tournament on the first Friday we have 24 teams of 3 compete in a game to 21. 1s and 2s, shot clock, running game clock with no free throws and Winners ball(win by 1). Ages 13 and up 5 dollar entry per person (cash at event) winning team takes home pot. For ages 12 and younger no entry fee with winning team will recieve 50 dollars each Sign Up

Basketball Skills Clinic

Every Saturday from "6 AM - 10 AM" developing basketball skills for players ages 6+. Coaches and experienced players will lead drills focusing on shooting, dribbling, passing, defense, and footwork. The clinic is designed to help athletes improve their game in a supportive and engaging environment. Sign Up

Open Gym

Every Saturday "4 PM - 12 AM" where basketball fans can enjoy a series of fun games, including open runs, a dunk contest, and a shooting challenge. This event brings the community together for a night of basketball under the lights, creating an energetic and exciting atmosphere with food and drinks as well as a live dj.
