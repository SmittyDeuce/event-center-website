About Us

Welcome to The Hardwood, the premier community basketball gym nestled in the heart of Louisiana's historic 7th Ward. Embodying our motto, "Blood, Sweat, and Buckets," we are dedicated to fostering a vibrant and inclusive environment where basketball enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels can come together to play, learn, and grow.
